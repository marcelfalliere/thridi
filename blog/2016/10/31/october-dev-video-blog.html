October dev video blog

Oct 31, 2016

In this way-too-long video, you’ll see what’s new since last month :

  • more objects supported by the hot-reload (material, mesh, geometry, light)
  • more properties supported by the hot-reload (geometry initial parameters, lights attributes, …)
  • global variables supported by hot-reload (almost)
  • toolbar buttons : pause, reload, wireframe and … share !
  • the thridi.io gallery
  • the discovery of a bug !

Still in a raw format (no editing, no nothing : just the software and me on the mic), I’m building a scene withs light and basic objects movement. At some point there’s a quirk - a bug. Discovered live ! But “c’est pas grave”, you know, the goal of this video is to inform you as authentically as possible.

Thridi is the missing editor for Three.js and WebGL. It has code reload. It respects the standards. It aims at making life painless for three.js developers.
